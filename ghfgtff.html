BMW M PHILOSOPHY

BMW M was created to transfer motorsport technology directly to road cars. Every M vehicle prioritizes mechanical balance, rear-wheel-drive character, limited-slip differentials, and driver control over raw straight-line numbers.

WHY BMW DOMINATES DRIFT CULTURE

BMW became the global benchmark for drifting due to near-perfect 50:50 weight distribution, predictable chassis behavior, smooth power delivery, and rear-wheel-drive platforms. These characteristics allow controlled oversteer, long sustained slides, and precise transitions.
